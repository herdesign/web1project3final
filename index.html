I’m a 20 year-old (visual communication designer). My name is Hailey Rodriguez and I am a student of Eastern Washington University. My interest in the artistic fields started at a young age with painting, drawing, sewing and crochet. That interest then molded into my love for design, after taking a Digital Arts and Filmmaking class at Tri-Tech, a trade school in the Tri-cities. I started collecting things like packaging, magazine articles, and screenshots of websites and apps. I would study these pieces and try to figure out why I found them appealing, what makes them stand out to me among everything else on the shelf or on the web. This is what led me to the Visual Communications field of the design world. I want to make things that draw people in and that stand out from the others.

Gallery

“A Fun Accident”

This piece was a personal project that I was working on. Something happened and a weird selection happened that looked interesting so I took a screenshot of it. It doesn’t have a purpose right now, but I love little glitches and oddities that happen during the process of creating and designing. Even weird mistakes can be beautiful.

“The Concepts of Type for Albums”

These two pieces are from the same collection. The prompt was to constrict oneself to a typeface and make an album cover based on limitations. The first one had no limitations and the second was two sizes two weights.

“Animals?”

These two are the photoshop collection I did. They combine an animal with a fruit and a flower to create an image that can be used for an advertisement, a fun poster, or a book cover.

“Spotify Card”

This a card for a spotify playlists. It’s a concept idea from a collection. This one is the one I’m most proud of.

“Concept Poster”

This is a concept for a project I am working on. The design is not complete, but it is a good view of what I can think up and begin creating within an hour.

“Confident”

The prompt for this piece was to take a typeface and make it express one of the words in a list of descriptive words. The typeface I chose is called Neuton. The typeface has an ExtraBold style. It was a fun style with some traditional elements that I enjoyed the look of visually.

“Cute Horror”

This is a set of icons that can be used for pin designs, sticker designs and for web icons. There are movie references to the Reanimator, Trick ‘r Treat, and the shining.
